# Software Services Agreement

## Section 1: Parties

This agreement is entered into between AlphaTech Solutions ("Vendor") and Global Finance Corp ("Client").

## Section 2: Contract Duration

The agreement begins on January 1, 2026 and ends on December 31, 2026.

## Section 3: Payment Terms

The Client shall pay $50,000 quarterly.

## Section 4: Data Security

The Vendor must maintain industry-standard security controls and report any data breach within 72 hours.

## Section 5: Termination

Either party may terminate the agreement with 30 days written notice.

## Section 6: Liability

Vendor liability is limited to the total contract value.
